Who We Are

A Producer-Led Exporter.
Sul de Minas.

Olga Mae is a producer-led exporter. We are coffee producers ourselves, and we work alongside a trusted community of neighbouring producers in Sul de Minas, Minas Gerais, Brazil, one of the world's most respected Arabica regions.

We do not act as a marketplace or intermediary between buyers and farmers. We represent our region and export its coffees directly, with full traceability, consistent quality, and professional export documentation.

"Every lot we export is traceable to its origin. That is not a promise, it is our operating standard."

Why Buy Direct

The Advantage of Buying at Origin

When you buy direct from origin, you deal with the producers, not a broker or warehouse. Better price, better traceability, and a direct relationship.

01
Better Price

No broker margins. You deal directly with the producers, which means competitive pricing with full transparency on costs.

02
Full Traceability

You know the producer, the harvest date, the processing method. This is what specialty buyers demand, and we deliver it.

03
Consistent Quality

We maintain consistent quality standards across every shipment, not just the first one.

04
Gulf Market Knowledge

We understand what Gulf buyers need, the standards, volumes, and communication that buyers in UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar and Oman require.

05
Samples First

We encourage you to cup before committing to a container. Samples are available on request, because we stand behind every lot we export.

06
Professional Export Standards

We manage export documentation, logistics and communication to ensure every transaction meets international standards.
